Digital technology

Year 11 digital tech has been one of my favourite subjects so far in all my years in school. I love how it challenges me to think in a different way that stimulates the problem-solving parts of my brain. This year, one of the most valuable skill I've learnt is how to code in phyton, even though its just the basics.

There's this quote I like: "trust the process". Its been so "gen-z-ed" these days, but I genuinely trusted the process when I started out and didn't know a single thing and even felt behind at times.

I might've overcomplicated things for my phyton assesment... so I only got a merit at the end TvT. But I really had fun with it and developed a good understanding of the nature of the code that I wouldn't have been able to if I didn't experiment and try a bunch of new, challenging stuff.
